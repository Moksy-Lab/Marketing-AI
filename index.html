FAQ's

What is the duration of the bootcamp?

The bootcamp runs for 8 weeks, covering essential and advanced digital marketing strategies, tools, and hands-on projects. You will have access to live sessions, recorded materials, and mentorship throughout the program.

Do I need prior experience in digital marketing?

No, prior experience is not required. The bootcamp is designed for both beginners and professionals, offering step-by-step guidance from fundamental concepts to advanced marketing strategies.

Will I receive a certification after completing the bootcamp?

Yes! Upon successfully completing the bootcamp and required assessments, you will receive an industry-recognized certification from Marketing.ai. This certification can enhance your resume and career prospects.

Will I work on real-life projects after completing the bootcamp?

Yes! The bootcamp includes hands-on projects during the course. After completion, you’ll have the opportunity to work on real-life case studies and build portfolio projects, helping you showcase your skills to potential employers or clients.

What if I want to enroll in multiple courses?

If you enroll in multiple courses, you may be eligible for a bundled discount. Contact our support team for details on special pricing and customized learning plans.
